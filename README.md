Diamonds Dashboard

It provides real-time insights into diamond quality (cut, clarity, color, carat), pricing trends, and customer demand. 

Key insights and features

•	Cut Analysis: Show how proportions, symmetry, and polish affect brilliance and sparkle.

•	Clarity Distribution: Visualize inclusions and blemishes across inventory, highlighting rarity and pricing impact.

•	Colour Grading Trends: Track diamonds by GIA colour scale (D–Z), showing demand for near colourless vs fancy colours.

•	Carat Weight Impact: Correlate carat size with price brackets and customer preferences.

•	Price vs Quality: Combine all 4Cs to reveal how each factor influences overall valuation.

•	Market Demand Insights: Identify which combinations (e.g., 1 carat, VS1 clarity, G colour) are most popular.

•	Inventory Health: Spot overstocked or understocked categories by cut, clarity, colour, and carat.
